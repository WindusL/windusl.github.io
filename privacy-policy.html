This App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since this App does not collect any personal data, it does not involve the processing of children's data.

Data Security

Since this App does not collect any personal data, there is no risk of data breach. All in-app purchase records are stored only on your local device.
